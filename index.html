How to play

  • Select exactly 4 tiles, then press Submit.
  • We’ll tell you how many of the 4 belong to the same hidden group (0–4).
  • Find all 4 groups before you make 4 mistakes.
  • When you solve a group, it moves to the top and locks in with Ducks-themed colors.
